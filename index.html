I am a PhD student in the Knowledge in AI group at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. My research focuses on knowledge representation and engineering for smart diagnosis of cyber-physical systems. This is part of ZORRO project which is a research project funded by NWO and carried out by a multidisciplinary consortium consisting of Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam, University of Twente,Saxion, TNO-ESI and industrial partners ASML, Canon Production Printers, ITEC, Philips, and ThermoFisher Scientific.
